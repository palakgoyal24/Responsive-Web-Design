One of the first things you need to do when you reach UK is to collect your BRP. It can collected from the Guildford Post Office which is situated at the town centre. All you need to do is get into a bus untill 'The Friary Centre' and walk your way to the Post Office.

Timings :-
	Monday: 9:00am to 5:30pm
	Tuesday: 9:30am to 5:30pm
	Wednesday: 9:00am to 5:30pm
	Thursday: 9:00am to 5:30pm
	Friday: 9:00am to 5:30pm
	Saturday: 9:00am to 5:30pm

Location : Click the button to get Post Office position.
